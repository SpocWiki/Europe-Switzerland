## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Courrendlin** (French pronunciation: [kuʁɑ̃dlɛ̃]; Franc-Comtois: Coérrendlïn) 
> is a municipality in the district of Delémont in the canton of Jura in Switzerland. 
> 
> On 1 January 2019 the former municipalities of Rebeuvelier and Vellerat 
> merged into the municipality of Courrendlin.
>
