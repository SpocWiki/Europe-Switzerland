## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> La Brévine (French: [la bʁevin], Franco-Provençal: [la brəˈvəna]) 
> is a municipality in the Neuchâtel Canton in Switzerland. 
> 
> It is the largest village in its eponymous valley, Vallée de la Brévine. 
> The area is renowned for its complex microclimate, 
> which is often much colder than nearby locations.
>
