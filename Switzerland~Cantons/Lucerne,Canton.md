## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The canton of Lucerne (German: Kanton Luzern [ˈkantɔn luˈtsɛrn]; 
> Romansh: Chantun Lucerna; French: Canton de Lucerne; Italian: Cantone Lucerna) is a canton of Switzerland. 
> 
> It is located in the country's central, German-speaking part. 
> The population of the canton (as of 31 December 2020) is 416,347. 
> As of 2007, the population included 57,268 foreigners, 
> or about 15.8% of the total population. 
> 
> The cantonal capital is the city of Lucerne.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Canton%20of%20Lucerne)
