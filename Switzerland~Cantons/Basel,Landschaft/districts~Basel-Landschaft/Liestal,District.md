## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Liestal** (Alemannic German: [ˈliə̯ʃd̥l̩], Standard German: [ˈliːstal] ), 
> formerly spelled Liesthal, is the capital of Liestal District 
> and the canton of Basel-Landschaft in Switzerland, 17 km (11 mi) south of Basel.
> 
> Liestal is an industrial town with a cobbled-street Old Town.
> 
> The official language of Liestal is (the Swiss variety of Standard) German, 
> but the main spoken language is the 
> local variant of the Alemannic Swiss German dialect.
>
